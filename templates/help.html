Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI recommendation system work?

Our system uses advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques including TF-IDF vectorization and cosine similarity to analyze article content. It compares the text, keywords, and summaries to find articles with similar themes and topics.

What do the similarity scores mean?

Similarity scores range from 0% to 100%. Higher scores indicate more similar content. A score of 80%+ means very similar articles, 60-80% means moderately similar, and below 60% means loosely related content.

How can I filter recommendations?

You can filter by category (Technology, Business, Politics, etc.) and sentiment (Positive, Negative, Neutral). Use the filter options in the search interface to narrow down your results.

Why are some articles not found?

Articles must be in our database to be found. Try using different keywords or check the spelling. The system searches through article titles, so be specific with your search terms.

How accurate are the recommendations?

Our AI model achieves high accuracy through content-based analysis. However, recommendations are based on content similarity, so personal preferences may vary. Use similarity scores to gauge relevance.

Can I use the system on mobile devices?

Yes! Our interface is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. The search and recommendation features are optimized for touch interfaces.

Tips & Tricks

Combine Filters

Use both category and sentiment filters together to find exactly what you're looking for.

Check Reading Time

Use the reading time estimates to plan your reading session and choose articles that fit your schedule.

High Similarity Scores

Focus on articles with 70%+ similarity scores for the most relevant recommendations.

Use Dark Mode

Toggle dark mode for comfortable reading in low-light environments or to reduce eye strain.

Try Different Searches

If you don't find what you're looking for, try different keywords or search terms to discover new articles.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

  • No search results: Try different keywords or check spelling
  • Slow loading: Check your internet connection
  • Page not loading: Refresh the browser or clear cache
  • Recommendations seem off: Try articles with higher similarity scores

Solutions

  • Browser issues: Try Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
  • Mobile problems: Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Search not working: Wait a few seconds and try again
  • Performance issues: Close other browser tabs
